Privacy Policy for Wakeit

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Wakeit collects the following information to provide alarm clock functionality:

  • Alarm settings and preferences (times, missions, repeat schedules)
  • Subscription status and purchase history
  • Device information necessary for app functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide alarm clock functionality and mission completion tracking
  • Process subscription payments via Superwall
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send local notifications for alarms

3. Third-Party Services

Wakeit uses the following third-party services:

  • Superwall: Payment processing and subscription management
  • Apple App Store: Subscription management and in-app purchases

These services have their own privacy policies and terms of service.

4. Data Storage

Alarm data is stored locally on your device. Subscription information is managed by Apple and Superwall in accordance with their privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Cancel your subscription at any time through the App Store

7. Children's Privacy

Wakeit is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
